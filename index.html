A Brief History of Fini

Fini Golosinas, S.A. is a Spanish confectionery company founded in 1970 by José Antonio Martínez Sánchez. The company is headquartered in Murcia, Spain, and has a workforce of over 700 employees. Fini is a leading manufacturer of gummy bears and other confectionery products, with a presence in over 100 countries worldwide.


Wacky Wormy Facts

  • Fini's first facility in Spain was a tiny 400-square-feet!
  • Fini makes over 200 different types of candies!
  • Fini's factories produce a whopping 70 million pounds of candy every year.
  • Fini Minhocas (sour worms) are one of their top sellers.
  • Fini candies are enjoyed in over 100 countries!
